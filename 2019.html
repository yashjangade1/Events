Flagship Events

Communiqué also conduct various events to help KGPians explore other sectors like MBA, UPSC, etc. Communiqué  has organised panel discussion to put limelight on these sectors. We brought panels who have aced in that respective domain to help clear doubts and provide some valuable guidance to how to ace exams and interviews. Here is the list of the events:

hidayat

On the eve of 65th Convocation, Communique and Student Welfare Group presented "Hidayat", a panel discussion by the glorious batch of 2019. The main purpose of the event was to make the first years aware of and learn from the valuable escapades shared by the esteemed panellists on all aspects of KGP Life.  

soft skills bootcamp

Communiqué: The Official Soft Skills Society, successfully conducted the Soft Skills Bootcamp to aid Final and Pre-Final Year Students. Personalized feedback regarding group discussions was provided to all candidates and a wide spectrum of common interview question answers were discussed. The students were given suggestion to improve fluency, vocabulary and overall personality.

Higher Education Seminar 

Communiqué: The Official Skills Society conducted a Higher Studies Seminar in association with Wallick Consulting where we hosted Ms. Heather Wallick( Ex- Admissions Officer at Harvard Law School). Attendees thoroughly enjoyed the insightful session, where all aspects of higher studies were discussed at length. 

Career training on upsc

A panel discussion with Scope IAS representative and Mr. Amit Kumar(AIR-91, UPSC, CSE 2018) on the resources and pre-requisites for the Civil Services Examination, followed by a one-to-one question and answer session where aspirants could directly ask their queries to Mr. Amit Kumar. 

Career Building Workshop

Workshop in which 2nd year students were Introduced to an array of career options, making CVs, writing cover letters and using Open Source and web tools to the best use.Communiqué collaborated with CAREERBUILD as a part of Assimilation Program, 2019 to help sophomores get a complete corporate career guidance workshop.
