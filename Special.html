Collaborative Events

Communiqué has organized many events collaborating with leading companies in the field of Education and Communication. Not only for the KGPians but also for the whole Indian student community, Communiqué has always been there to organize some innovative and brainstorming events. Here are few of the events Communiqué has organized in association with leading companies.

Getting Yourself Published

Communiqué in association with WiseUp Communications organised an enlightening session on How to effectively write a Research Article with Founder Neha Agrawal, an alumnus of NTU Singapore. The session covered importance of scientific writing, organising research article sections, how to write an abstract and avoid plagiarism. Registrations – 400+

Mock GD/PI Session

Communiqué in association with Erudite organised a session that will cover major aspects of Group Discussions and Personal Interviews, giving the best chance to know and correct mistakes from the experts and giving the benefit of personalized feedback from the experts. The session helped Immensely to our final year students.
Registrations – 130+

Campus Challenge

Communiqué in association with American Express organised the Campus Challenge 2021 for the final year students. The first 10 qualifying teams to submit a solution got Amazon Gift Vouchers worth Rs 1000 and a chance to sit in a Pre-Placement interview during Campus Placements.
Registrations – 270+

Crackathon

Communiqué introduces the Business Case Crackathon, with the esteemed organization Johnson & Johnson, a conglomerate working with countless successful professionals from the field of business analysis. The launch webinar was hosted by field experts launching the competition by releasing the problem statement.
Registrations – 110+
Winners – 1. Team Mavericks
                   2. Team Bytefourz

GPAT

After the astounding performance by KGP Community in Johnson and Johnson's Crackathon, Communiqué is back with another challenge - Geek Placement Assessment Test (GPAT) in association with GeeksforGeeks, free of cost contest designed to test our skills against industry requirements for all KGPians. The test covered Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Ability and Programming Logical and Theoretical.
Registrations – 310+

Young Leaders Programme

Communiqué, in association with the Indian School of Business, one of the most renowned Business Schools of India, organised a session on the Young Leaders' Program to understand and discuss the importance of MBA in today's world and in India, and share insights on helping college students plan their careers even before they graduate.
Registrations – 150+

Product Deck Making Masterclass

Communiqué IIT Kharagpur organised a session Product Deck Making MasterClass in collaboration with Doremon Den which covered "actionable steps" to make a deck required for Case Study Assignments in most placements and Internships product roles.
Registrations – 210+

All India Under 21 Case Study

MissionEd, in association with Communiqué, conducted an All India Under 21 Case study to solve some of the most complex problems in the community-based platform of the education sector, with a prize pool of worth 1 lakh+. 
Registrations – 120+
Winners – Team Four and a Half Consultant, Team Somehow We Manage
